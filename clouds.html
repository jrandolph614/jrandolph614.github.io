Cloudiness

This graph displays a cities latitude value versus the cloudiness. Looking at the data it can be seen that between the degrees of 40-70 higher levels of cloudiness were recorded. While latitude values less than 0 degrees show lower levels of cloudiness. This can be answered by that it generally rains more in the northern hemisphere than the southern.
